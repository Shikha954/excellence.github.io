OUR INSTITUTIONS

Founded in year 1999, Excellence Group of Institutions is one of the Central India's largest and oldest Educational Group led by a team of IIT, IIM and Stanford University Alumni. Technocrats Group of Institution comprises of 4 Engineering, 1 Management, 2 Pharmacy, 1 Commerce Law & Sciences and 1 Flim and Design institutes with about 16,000+ students and 1,326+ faculty members on campus, offering courses from Undergraduate upto Doctoral level. The group has MoUs with University of Cincinnati, University of Central Missouri, Tokyo Institute of Technology for research, King Mongkut’s University of Technology, Namibia University of Science and Technology and California Baptist University, academics as well as student exchange programs. The group also has Innovation Centers of Bosch, Dassault Systems, RedHat, Oracle, AWS, Bentley, Mitsubishi Electric, Siemens, Google and Texas Instruments to prepare students for industry. The group is the largest Technical Educational Group in Central India to have NBA Accreditation. The group was awarded as the "Best Engineering Institute in Central India" by Smriti Irani Hon'ble Minister of Women and Child Development, Govt. of India, at ASSOCHAM Summit & Awards 2019. The major recruiters that visited Technocrats Group last year included SAP, E&Y, Goldman Sachs, S&P Global, Amazon, Paytm, IBM, Capgemini, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Mphasis, Hexaware, Virtusa, HCL, Ashok Leyland, Tech Mahindra, TATA Motors, TATA Hitachi, SANY, Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger and many more.
